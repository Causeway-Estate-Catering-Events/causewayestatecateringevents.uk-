Welcome to The Causeway

Located in the heart of Herefordshire Golf Club, The Causeway Cafe & Bar has been a cornerstone of the community since 1932. Our establishment combines rich heritage with modern hospitality, offering a welcoming atmosphere for both golfers and visitors alike.

Our Values

Quality

We pride ourselves on serving fresh, locally-sourced ingredients prepared with care and attention to detail.

Tradition

With over 90 years of history, we maintain the highest standards while embracing modern culinary trends.

Community

We're more than just a cafe - we're a gathering place for the local community and golf club members.

Welcome to The Causeway Estate Catering

Nestled within the prestigious Herefordshire Golf Club, The Causeway Estate's Catering services offer an exceptional dining experience in a setting steeped in history and tradition.

Our menu celebrates locally-sourced ingredients, featuring everything from hearty breakfast classics to elegant afternoon teas, complemented by our carefully curated selection of wines and beverages.

Whether you're a golfer looking for post-round refreshments or a visitor seeking a memorable dining experience, our warm hospitality and stunning views create the perfect atmosphere for any occasion.
